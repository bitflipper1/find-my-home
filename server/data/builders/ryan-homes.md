## Questions, risks, and next actions

- "Cut corners, cheapest contractors" is the single most repeated negative theme across independent platforms for this builder — an independent inspection contingency is advisable regardless of which Ryan Homes community is under consideration.
- Always obtain a competing mortgage quote before assuming an NVR-Mortgage-tied incentive is a net financial win after accounting for any rate difference.
- Sunset Creek Townhomes pricing (from $284,990) was not independently re-verified against a current builder listing in this pass — confirm current price and incentive structure directly before treating it as actionable.
