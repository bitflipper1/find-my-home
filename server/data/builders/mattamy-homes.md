## Questions, risks, and next actions

- This is one of only two builders in this research set (with Eastwood Homes) where Charlotte-specific review data was directly located, rather than only national aggregate data — the negative signal here is more locally grounded than for most other builders in this set and should be weighted accordingly.
- Given the specific "warranty office is impossible to reach" complaint pattern, get a named warranty-department contact and escalation path in writing before closing on a Sugar Creek unit.
- Note the SC investor-tax caution already flagged elsewhere in this app applies to Sugar Creek (Indian Land, SC) — combine with this reputation risk when evaluating that specific lead.
