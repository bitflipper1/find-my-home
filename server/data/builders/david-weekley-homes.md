## Questions, risks, and next actions

- The gap between weak buyer reviews (2.9/5) and strong employee reviews (4.4/5) suggests inconsistent field/subcontractor execution rather than a management problem — worth probing directly with the sales rep about which subcontractors serve this specific community.
- Confirm in writing whether the model-home warranty truly starts from original CO date or from the buyer's closing date — this profile currently treats that as an unconfirmed assumption, not a verified fact.
- A second model in this builder's Charlotte portfolio was referenced informally (buyer's own inbox, not a public source) — treat as a lead requiring direct confirmation, not a verified fact.
